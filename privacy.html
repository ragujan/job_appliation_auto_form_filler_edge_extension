JobFill AI — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2, 2026
Last updated: July 2, 2026

JobFill AI (“we”, “our”, or “the extension”) is a browser extension that helps you auto-fill job application forms, track applications, and tailor your resume using AI. This policy explains what information the extension handles and how it is used.

We do not operate a backend server. Your data is stored on your device unless you choose to send it to a third-party AI provider (see Section 4).

1. Information we handle

Information you provide

When you use JobFill AI, you may store:

  • Profile data — name, email, phone, address, work history, links, cover letter text, and other fields you load or enter
  • Resume content — text or PDF files you upload
  • Job application records — job titles, companies, URLs, status, and notes
  • AI settings — your chosen provider, model, and API key (optional)
  • Extension preferences — auto-fill settings, backup options, and similar toggles

Information read from web pages (only when you take action)

The extension does not run on every website automatically. It only accesses a web page when you trigger an action, such as:

  • Fill Form Now
  • Detect from Page (job description)
  • Track This Application / Refresh on the Applications tab

When triggered, the extension may read form fields, job descriptions, and page metadata (e.g. job title, company name, URL) only on the tab you are actively using to perform that action.

2. How we use your information

We use your information solely to provide extension features:

Feature Use
Auto-fill Match your profile data to form fields on the current page
Application tracking Save and display your job search records locally
Resume tailoring Send your resume and job description to your chosen AI provider when you click Tailor Resume with AI
Backup / export Save copies of your data locally in browser storage or a folder you choose

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties.

3. Where your data is stored

Browser storage (default)

By default, all data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome/Edge extension storage (chrome.storage.local). It stays on your device and is not uploaded to us.

Optional local folder backup

If you enable folder backup, the extension can write JSON files to a folder you select on your computer. We do not access that folder except when you choose backup, sync, or import actions.

Optional export / import

You may export or import backup files manually. Those files remain under your control.

4. Third-party AI providers

If you use Tailor Resume with AI, the extension sends data directly from your browser to the AI provider you configure, such as:

  • Anthropic (Claude)
  • OpenAI
  • Google Gemini
  • OpenRouter
  • Ollama (local, on your machine)
  • A custom OpenAI-compatible endpoint you specify

What may be sent: your resume (text or PDF), the job description you provide or detect, and your API credentials (as required by that provider’s API).

When it is sent: only when you click Tailor Resume with AI. Nothing is sent for auto-fill or application tracking.

Each provider has its own privacy policy and terms. You are responsible for choosing a provider you trust and for your API usage and costs.

We do not receive, store, or process your AI requests on our own servers.

5. What we do not collect

JobFill AI does not:

  • Collect analytics or usage telemetry
  • Use cookies or tracking pixels on a website we operate (this policy page is static)
  • Sell or rent your personal information
  • Automatically upload your profile, resume, or applications to our servers

6. Permissions

The extension requests browser permissions only to function:

  • Storage — save your profile, settings, and applications locally
  • Active tab & scripting — interact with the page you are on when you click an action
  • Tabs — identify the active tab you are working with
  • Side panel — display the extension interface

The extension does not request broad access to all websites (<all_urls>). Page access occurs only when you initiate an action on the current tab.

7. Data retention and deletion

Your data remains on your device until you delete it. You can:

  • Clear profile data in the extension (Clear All on the Auto-Fill tab)
  • Delete individual tracked applications
  • Remove your API key in Settings
  • Export then delete backup files from your folder
  • Remove the extension from your browser (this removes locally stored extension data)

Uninstalling the extension deletes data stored in browser extension storage. Data in an optional backup folder you created is not automatically deleted — remove those files manually if desired.

8. Security

Your API key and personal data are stored locally in your browser. Optional backups may include your API key if you enable Include API key in backups. Keep backup files private.

No method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Use the extension only on devices you trust.

9. Children’s privacy

JobFill AI is not intended for users under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the extension after changes means you accept the updated policy.
